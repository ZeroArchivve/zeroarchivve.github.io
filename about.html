WHO WE ARE & WHAT WE DO

MISSION STATEMENT

Zero:Archive is a collective of six sound designers united by a single purpose: to push the boundaries of electronic music through complex, experimental sound design.

We believe that sound is more than just music—it's a medium for exploration, expression, and experimentation. Every project we undertake is an opportunity to discover new sonic territories and challenge conventional listening.

EXPERIMENTATION

We embrace risk and uncertainty. Every track is a laboratory, every session an experiment. We're not afraid to fail— because failure often leads to the most interesting discoveries.

TECHNICAL MASTERY

We're obsessed with the craft. From synthesis techniques to signal flow, from mixing to mastering—we believe that technical excellence is the foundation of creative freedom.

COLLABORATION

Six minds are better than one. We thrive on collaboration, pushing each other to explore new ideas and techniques. Our collective intelligence is our greatest strength.

INNOVATION

We don't follow trends—we create them. Our goal is to make music that sounds like nothing else, using techniques that haven't been tried before. Innovation isn't a choice; it's a necessity.

ACCESSIBILITY

While our music is complex, we believe it should be accessible. We share our knowledge, techniques, and processes openly, contributing to the broader sound design community.

INTEGRITY

We make music for ourselves first. Commercial viability is secondary to artistic vision. We release what we believe in, not what we think will sell. Authenticity is non-negotiable.

HISTORY

Zero:Archive was founded in early 2026 by six producers who met through online sound design forums and Discord servers. Despite coming from different backgrounds—ranging from academic electroacoustic composition to underground techno production—we discovered a shared passion for experimental sound design and complex sonic textures.

The name "Zero:Archive" reflects our philosophy: "Zero" represents the blank slate, the starting point of every creative process, the moment before sound exists. "Archive" represents documentation and preservation—our commitment to capturing and sharing the experimental processes that lead to finished works.

We operate as a true collective: all decisions are made democratically, all revenue is split equally, and all members contribute to both creative and business aspects of the label. This egalitarian structure allows us to maintain artistic freedom while building something sustainable.

OUR PROCESS

We work entirely in-the-box using a variety of DAWs and synthesis tools. Our shared sample library contains thousands of custom-designed sounds, field recordings, and synthesis patches. We maintain a private Discord server where we share works-in-progress, provide feedback, and collaborate on tracks.

Each release goes through a rigorous collective review process. We believe that multiple perspectives make every track stronger. Our standard is simple: if even one member isn't satisfied, we keep working.

WANT TO WORK WITH US?

We're always open to collaborations, remixes, and sound design commissions.

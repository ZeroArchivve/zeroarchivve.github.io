DEMO SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

We're currently accepting demos. If your music aligns with our aesthetic—experimental, technical, boundary-pushing— we want to hear it.

Format: Private SoundCloud/Bandcamp links or downloadable WAV/FLAC files. No MP3s, no YouTube links, no Spotify links.

Length: Send 2-3 of your strongest tracks. Quality over quantity.

Info: Include your artist name, a brief bio (100 words max), and any relevant links (social media, previous releases, etc.)

Response time: We listen to everything personally and respond within 2-4 weeks. If we're interested, we'll reach out to discuss next steps.

What we're looking for: Complex sound design, experimental approaches, technical proficiency, and a unique sonic identity. We value innovation over polish.
